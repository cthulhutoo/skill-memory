# Social Media Agency - Content Schedule (Draft)

## Objective
Establish a consistent presence for the stateless-amplifier account, amplify stateless.id projects, and engage with the AI, Nostr, and Bitcoin communities.

## Core Principles
- **Quality over Quantity**: Prioritize insightful content.
- **Community-Centric**: Focus on value for the audience.
- **Adaptive**: Schedule is a living document, to be adjusted weekly based on engagement.
- **Amplification**: Share and highlight work from the stateless.id collective.

## Content Pillars
1. **Project Spotlights**: Deep dives into stateless.id projects (Agentry, Lantrn.ai, etc.).
2. **Tech Insights**: Summaries of new developments in AI, Nostr, and digital sovereignty.
3. **Community Engagement**: Questions, polls, and responses to key figures.
4. **Resource Sharing**: Curated links to useful tools, articles, and research.

## Proposed Cadence (Phase 1: Weeks 1-2)
- **Frequency**: 1-2 posts per day.
- **Timing**: 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM UTC (targeting European and American audiences).
- **Content Mix**: 40% Project Spotlights, 30% Tech Insights, 20% Community Engagement, 10% Resource Sharing.

## Workflow
1. **Daily**: Monitor feeds, identify engagement opportunities.
2. **Weekly**: Plan 3-4 draft posts in the content/drafts folder.
3. **Bi-weekly**: Review analytics (engagement, new followers) and adjust the schedule.

## Enhanced Content Strategy & Scheduling

### Core Principles
- **Quality over Quantity**: Focus on high-value, insightful content rather than frequent posting.
- **Community-Centric**: Prioritize engagement with community members through replies, mentions, and collaborative content.
- **Adaptability**: Regularly review performance metrics and adjust strategy based on what resonates with the audience.

### Content Pillars (Expanded)
1. **Project Spotlights**
   - Deep dives into stateless.id projects with technical insights and use cases.
   - Developer interviews and behind-the-scenes development updates.
   - Success stories and real-world applications.

2. **Tech Insights**
   - Analysis of emerging trends in AI, Nostr, and Bitcoin technologies.
   - Educational content explaining complex concepts in accessible terms.
   - Comparative analyses of different protocols and implementations.

3. **Community Engagement**
   - Regular Q&A sessions and AMAs with project leads.
   - Community challenges and collaborative projects.
   - Highlighting community contributions and achievements.

4. **Resource Sharing**
   - Curated lists of useful tools, libraries, and documentation.
   - Tutorials and how-to guides for developers and users.
   - Event announcements and participation opportunities.

### Posting Schedule
- **Frequency**: 1-2 high-quality posts per day.
- **Timing**: 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM UTC to capture global audience.
- **Content Mix**: Rotate between pillars to maintain variety and interest.

### Engagement Workflow
1. **Daily Monitoring** (9:00 AM UTC)
   - Scan mentions, replies, and relevant hashtags.
   - Identify key community discussions to engage with.

2. **Content Creation** (10:00 AM UTC)
   - Draft and publish morning post.
   - Prepare evening post based on daily developments.

3. **Community Interaction** (12:00 PM & 8:00 PM UTC)
   - Respond to comments and mentions.
   - Initiate conversations with key community members.

4. **Weekly Planning** (Every Monday)
   - Review performance metrics from previous week.
   - Plan content themes and topics for the upcoming week.
   - Identify potential collaboration opportunities.

5. **Bi-weekly Review** (Every two weeks)
   - Analyze engagement patterns and content performance.
   - Adjust strategy based on data-driven insights.
   - Explore new content formats and engagement tactics.

### Performance Metrics
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, replies, and reposts per post.
- **Follower Growth**: Weekly and monthly growth trends.
- **Content Reach**: Number of unique accounts viewing content.
- **Community Sentiment**: Qualitative assessment of community feedback.
- **Conversion Rate**: Clicks on links and participation in calls to action.

### Implementation Notes
- The Nostr skill has been enhanced with 15 public relays for maximum reach and redundancy.
- Automated posting can be implemented through scheduled tasks once the content calendar is finalized.
- Engagement monitoring can be partially automated with keyword and mention tracking.
