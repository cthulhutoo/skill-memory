## Content Strategy
### Core Principles
- Quality over Quantity: Focus on high-value, insightful content.
- Community-Centric: Prioritize engagement with community members.
- Adaptability: Regularly review metrics and adjust strategy.

### Content Pillars
1. **Project Spotlights**: Deep dives into stateless.id projects, developer interviews, success stories.
2. **Tech Insights**: Analysis of emerging trends, educational content, comparative analyses.
3. **Community Engagement**: Q&A sessions, community challenges, highlighting contributions.
4. **Resource Sharing**: Curated tools, tutorials, event announcements.

### Posting Schedule
- Frequency: 1-2 high-quality posts per day.
- Timing: 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM UTC to capture global audience.
- Content Mix: Rotate between pillars to maintain variety.

## Engagement Workflow
1. **Daily Monitoring** (9:00 AM UTC): Scan mentions, replies, and hashtags.
2. **Content Creation** (10:00 AM UTC): Draft and publish posts.
3. **Community Interaction** (12:00 PM & 8:00 PM UTC): Respond to comments and initiate conversations.
4. **Weekly Planning** (Every Monday): Review metrics and plan content.
5. **Bi-weekly Review** (Every two weeks): Analyze performance and adjust strategy.

## Performance Metrics
- Engagement Rate: Likes, replies, and reposts per post.
- Follower Growth: Weekly and monthly trends.
- Content Reach: Unique accounts viewing content.
- Community Sentiment: Qualitative assessment of feedback.
- Conversion Rate: Clicks on links and participation in calls to action.
